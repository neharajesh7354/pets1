Animals!

Dogs

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. There are sevelral hundred breeds of domesticated and wild dogs.
Dogs were the first animal that was tamed by human over 22,000 years ago. Dogs are also the most popular pet in the world.
A third of all households in the US contain a Dog. They are loyal friendly pets and loved by many in the Us.

Cats

A cat belongs to the family Felidae. Feral cats are cats that were born in the wild or domesticated cats that have returned to the wild.
When a cat is pleased and contented it willlet out a purrung noise. The earliest archaeological evidence for domestic cats was found in Cyprus and dates back approximately 9,500 years. Domestic cats usually weigh around 8 pounds (4 kilograms) to 11 pounds (5 kilograms).
